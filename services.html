Base Oils
Base oils reduce friction and wear, ensuring equipment performance and longevity. Premium oils like SN150, SN500, and SN900 enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and improve durability.
02
Oil & Gas Engineering
Premium Motor Spirit (PMS), derived from crude oil, ensures efficient combustion, improved engine performance, fuel efficiency, and reduced emissions across various applications.
03
Automotive Gas Oil
Automotive Gas Oil (AGO) provides high energy density, clean combustion, and reduced engine wear, ensuring efficient performance and reliability in transportation, construction, and agricultural machinery.
04
High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE)
High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) provides strength, durability, chemical resistance, and recyclability, making it ideal for packaging, construction, and consumer goods.
05
Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE)
Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible, lightweight, and resistant to chemicals, ideal for packaging, agriculture, and industrial applications requiring stretchability, durability, and cost-efficiency.
06
Polypropylene (PP)
Polypropylene (PP) is a durable, chemical-resistant, and versatile thermoplastic, ideal for automotive, medical, packaging, and consumer goods, offering exceptional strength, flexibility, high-temperature resistance, and long-lasting performance.
07
Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Polypropylene
Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is durable, recyclable, and versatile, ideal for packaging, textiles, and electronics, offering strength, sustainability, and excellent barrier properties for food and beverages.
08
Blow Molding Accessories
Blow molding accessories ensure precision and efficiency in producing hollow plastic products, enhancing quality with durable molds, machines, and valves for consistent, high-performance manufacturing..
